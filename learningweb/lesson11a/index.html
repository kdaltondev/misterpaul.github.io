This is a half-liter (16.9 oz) water bottle. It contains about 10 grams of plastic. (Soda bottles are thicker, so they contain more plastic.)

Every week, you might eat about half of that!

In 2016, the world produced about 330 million metric tons of plastic. (2020 is estimated to be 367 million metric tons.) How much is that?

The math: A metric ton is 1000 kg, or 1,000,000 g, or 100,000 10-gram water bottles. Our 10 gram bottle is crushed to 4 pixels high x 60 pixels wide = 240 pixels, so this box is 2400 x 10,000 pixels
